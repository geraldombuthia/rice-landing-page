Who are we

Earthgrain is a premium yet affordable cooking rice that has been grown and cultivated in the agriculturally productive kenyan highlands. Its is cleaned and packaged using state-of-art technology to ensure retention of its aroma and freshness. EarthGrain rice is easy to cook and goes along with all available recipes.
